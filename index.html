Demo Training

Roleplay scenarios for demoing GoTo Connect to a small business customer.

These materials give Solution Consultants realistic, story-driven practice for demoing GoTo Connect. Each scenario follows Taylor — owner of Shear Bliss Salon — as his business grows and his phone needs evolve. The scenarios are designed to be run in sequence or individually. All scenarios follow the Demo2Win methodology: discovery before demo, features tied to outcomes, no jargon.

The 5 Scenarios
# Scenario Complexity Key Features
1 "We Just Use Our Cell Phones" Basic Business number, mobile app, voicemail greeting
2 "I Don't Know How Many Calls We Miss" Basic–Intermediate Call reporting, missed call log, voicemail-to-email
3 "Clients Keep Texting Our Personal Numbers" Intermediate Business SMS, shared team inbox, admin visibility
4 "We Need to Look More Professional" Intermediate–Advanced Auto-attendant, IVR, hold music, after-hours routing
5 "We're Opening a Second Location" Advanced Multi-location, overflow routing, user management, admin portal
Scoring

Use the Demo2Win Scoring Rubric to evaluate SC performance after each session. 13 criteria across 5 sections, 52 points max. Includes a debrief guide for Taylor to use after each roleplay.

How to Use These Materials

  1. Start with Scenario 1 for new SCs — even experienced reps benefit from the discovery discipline.
  2. Run discovery first. The SC must surface Taylor's pain through questions before showing any feature.
  3. Introduce objections after the first run of each scenario — not on the first attempt.
  4. Debrief with the rubric after each session. Pick one area to develop, not five.
  5. Scenario 5 as capstone: An SC who runs all five fluently is demo-ready.
